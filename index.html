Biology-led sensing and translation

Elisentra is a science-driven initiative focused on the design and translation of biological sensing systems across environmental and industrial contexts.

Our work spans biotechnology, bioengineering, and systems-level approaches for applications in water and wastewater, agriculture, mining, industry, and the built environment.

This site is intentionally minimal. Additional information will be released as research, collaborations, and programs mature.
